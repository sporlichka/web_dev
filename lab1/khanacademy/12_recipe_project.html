  1. Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta

Creamy garlic chicken pasta

  • Time: 30 min.
  • Serves: 2.
Ingredients Quantity
pasta 200g
chicken breast 300g
cream 100g
seasoning as much as you need

Step 1: Cut chicken breast into a tiny pieces and start to fry it on a pan
Step 2:boil pasta and don't forget to salt the water
Step 3:after breast is ready add cream and seasoning into pan and wait for 5-10 minutes with light fire, then add pasta
Step 4:Dish is ready!

Source: skinnyspatula
